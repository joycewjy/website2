At BoulderMovement,

We make it possible for you to stay active, healthy and strong as part of your of life.
Located at the heart of the CBD area, we offer a place to work and a place to meet your fitness goals through bouldering.

Bouldering...?
Bouldering is a sport that incorporates the whole body when scaling up to the top. It involves the knowledge, planning and application of a series of movement that is bound to make you stronger physically and mentally! Experience bouldering with a community that is passionate, friendly, and supportive. Whether you are just exploring for a day, or looking to pick up a new hobby that will shape your life for years to come, we have what you need and we can help you #climbtoabetteryou.
