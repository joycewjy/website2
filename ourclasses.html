Bouldering Basics

If you are just starting out on your bouldering journey, this class will equip you with the vital skills you need. It will cover body positioning and movement techniques to allow you to effectively use your upper body. There will be conditioning exercises for the core and legs.

Mobility through Bouldering

Mobility involves strentgh and flexibility. You may be strong overall, but tightness in the muscle may limit you from reaching your full potential. In bouldering, you would realise that you would need mobility! This class will bring you through mobility exercises that targets the whole body.

Great for anyone looking into overall fitness.

Bouldering Advanced
